Teaching

In the classroom I provide an atmosphere conducive to learning mathematics by alleviating any fear of mathematics hindering students from their success and by creating content accessible to those coming from areas other than mathematics. This approach to teaching has produced many happy and successful students, whose evaluations are available upon request for appropriate parties.
