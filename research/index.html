Research

My research interests lie in computational aspects of algebraic geometry. In particular, numerical and symbolic computation of Galois groups arising in enumerative problems are of interest.

  • Solving Decomposable Sparse Systems, Numerical Algorithms 88, 453-474 (2021)
  • Decomposable Sparse Polynomial Systems, Journal of Software for Algebra and Geometry Vol. 11 (2021), 53-59
    • Software for decomposable sparse polynomial systems.
  • Polyhedral Homotopies in Cox Coordinates
  • Galois Groups in Enumerative Geometry and Applications
